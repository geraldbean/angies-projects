Quotable

It all started in my dorm room...

Project Overview

When I realized my classmates around me were recording quotes I thought it would be cool to be able to centralize those quotes in one place. That was when I came up with the concept of Quotable. A digital quotebook where people can record, and share their favourite quotes with friends.

# Built a web application using React.
# Developed sharing quotes between multiple accounts, recording and editing quotes.
# Launched the platform as a start-up, and wrote a business plan, and pitch deck for marketing.
